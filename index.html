About Globetrader Rare Maps

Established in 2006 by proprietor Ken Klatt, Globetrader Rare Maps is a dealer in antique maps from the 16th to the 19th centuries. Antique maps are a great way to personalize your home decor and add an elegant design element to any room. We have a wide selection of fine maps for you every need.

We love working with retail shops and interior designers and offer our maps at wholesale prices to our valued partners.
